## üìä Key Insights Summary

* Inflation trends vary significantly by category
* 2020 inflation peak was driven by **supply shocks**, not excess demand
* Food inflation has a disproportionate impact due to high CPI weight
* Transport inflation closely tracks oil price movements
* Post-peak declines often reflect **base-effect dynamics** rather than true deflation

## üéØ Business & Policy Implications
